Bedrock Skin to Legacy Console Skin

Converts Bedrock skin geometry (.json) to a ".txt" that can be imported into PCK-Studio to be used in Legacy Console Edition. Note!: If the skin has a 128x128 texture not mapped to 64x64 geometry the UVs will be incorrect. Also, if the skin has geometry with perface UV it too will be incorrect.
